Gyros with Tzatziki

Gyros made out of pork and yogurt, cucumber Tzatziki

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Red Onions
  • Gyros Seasoning
  • Yogurt
  • Cucumber
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cut pork into small pieces.
  2. Cut red onions into small pieces.
  3. Season the pork and red onions with the gyros seasoning
  4. Cut cucumber and garlic into small pieces.
  5. Mix the cucumber and garlic with the yogurt.
  6. Braise the Gyros in a pan.
  7. Serve the Gyros on a plate with the Tzatziki.
